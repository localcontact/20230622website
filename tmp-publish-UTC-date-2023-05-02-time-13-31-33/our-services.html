OUR SERVICES

Residence Visa Services


The Spanish immigration system can be difficult to understand. Our professionals will manage all of the administrative and legal processes required to obtain a visa and begin your new life in Spain.


We will put you in touch with experienced individuals who will walk you through the process of acquiring your residency visa, including helping you through the necessary paperwork and navigating local bureaucracy.


The Local Contact provides assistance with:

  • Non Lucrative Visa
  • Digital Nomad Visa
  • Golden Visa
  • N.I.E. (Numero de identidad Extranjero - Foreigner Identity Number)
