WHO WE ARE

The Local Contact is a boutique, native English-speaking relocation services agency the focuses on helping clients relocate to the Costa Blanca. Our passion is helping people thrive in their new life in Spain.

At The Local Contact, we understand the challenges of relocating to a new country and we are committed to eliminating these obstacles for others. We take pride in being there for you every step of the way.

The Local Contact was born out of the frustrations that our founder, Margaret Nganga, experienced first-hand when trying to navigate the Spanish bureaucracy when her family were newcomers to Spain. We saw the need for a personalized, English-speaking service that could help others navigate the complexities of starting life in a new country.

With years of experience living in both Valencia and the Costa Blanca, our team of is dedicated to making the relocation process stress-free for our clients. We know that every client's situation is unique, and that's why we offer a wide range of services that can be customized to meet your specific needs.


Our team of local experts has years of experience living in both Valencia and the Costa Blanca, and is dedicated to making the relocation process stress-free for our clients.


We offer a wide range of services, including assistance with obtaining visas, opening bank accounts, finding a home, setting up utilities, navigating the local school system, cultural differences, and day-to-day life in a new country, and more.


We pride ourselves on delivering personalized attention and tailored solutions, taking the time to understand your unique situation and providing guidance every step of the way.


Thank you for considering The Local Contact for your relocation needs. We look forward to helping you create your best new life on the Costa Blanca.

Contact us today to start your journey!
