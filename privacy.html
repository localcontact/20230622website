PRIVACY POLICY


The Local Contact complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and as such, we are committed to protecting our users' privacy. The following Privacy Policy describes what types of personal information we gather, how we use it, and the safeguarding measures we take.



Information Collection and Use:
We collect personal information from our users in order to provide them with the services offered by The Local Contact. This information may include, but is not limited to, name, email address, and telephone number. We use this information to contact users in response to their inquiries, provide them with information about our services, and send them promotional materials.


Data Retention:
We will retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, or as required by law.


Data Security:
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures, as well as physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.


Data Access and Control:
You have the right to access and control the personal information that we collect. You can request to review, change, or delete your personal information by contacting us at the email address provided on our website.


Data Processing Agreement:
We have a data processing agreement in place with our service providers and any other third parties who process personal data on our behalf, which ensures that they comply with the GDPR.


Third-Party Websites:
Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such websites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our website and to read the privacy policies of each website they visit.


If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address provided on our website.


Thank you for using our website.
